Help

The goal is to get the ball to the green square!. Finishing a level increases your score by 50 points.

Try to avoid the fires, you will lose a life !! You only get 3 so be careful.

Picking up gold stars increases your score by 20, and slows your ball for a short while.

If you wish to reverse the x-axis/y-axis, or lock the view mode in portrait/landscape then please see the settings menu.
